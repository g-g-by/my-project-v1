New to guppies or looking to improve your breeding success? Our guides cover essential topics to help your Guppy Gems thrive.

Essential Water Parameters for Thriving Guppies

Maintaining stable and appropriate water conditions is paramount for guppy health and breeding. Guppies prefer slightly alkaline water with a pH of 7.0-8.0 and moderately hard water. Consistent temperature between 24-27°C (75-80°F) is ideal. Regular water changes and monitoring ammonia, nitrite, and nitrate levels are crucial.

Feeding Your Guppies for Optimal Health and Color

A varied diet is key. Offer high-quality flake or pellet food as a staple. Supplement with live or frozen foods like brine shrimp, daphnia, or bloodworms for extra nutrition and to enhance their vibrant colors. For fry, specialized fry food or finely crushed flakes are necessary. Feed small amounts multiple times a day.
